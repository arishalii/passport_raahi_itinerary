Frequently Asked Questions

How do I modify my active itinerary? expand_more

If your trip is active, you can write feedback comments directly on specific itinerary blocks. Our editors are notified in real-time and will adjust details immediately.

Why is my trip status still 'payment_pending'? expand_more

Our reservations require manual transaction validation. It usually takes 2–4 hours for our finance desk to update your status to active.

Can I request a custom flight transfer? expand_more

Yes, write an inquiry inside the concierge contact form on this page, or mention it directly in your trip dashboard comments drawer.
