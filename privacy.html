Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information Collection

At Passport Raahi, we collect necessary personal details to tailor bespoke luxury itineraries for you. This includes names, contact information, travel preferences, flight paths, and accommodation details. No payment credentials or credit cards are processed directly on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Your details are primarily used to construct itineraries, process bookings, coordinate with local luxury transportation partners, and customize real-time client itinerary dashboards. We do not sell or lease user data under any circumstances.

3. Cookies and State

We use functional cookies to handle active customer and editor sessions. These cookies are strictly configured to prevent third-party exposure and are expired appropriately on logout.

4. Security Measures

We employ advanced encryption algorithms (TLS/SSL) for all network requests. Your database information is held securely, utilizing Row Level Security protocols to verify your identity before accessing itineraries.
